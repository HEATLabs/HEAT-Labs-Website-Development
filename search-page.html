Your Privacy Matters

HEAT Labs is designed with your privacy as a top priority. The search queries you enter on this page never leave your device unless you press Enter to perform a search.

Local Storage Only: Your frequent searches are stored locally in your browser's memory (localStorage). This data is used solely to populate the "Frequent Searches" tags below the search bar. It is never exported, tracked, or sent to any HEAT Labs server.

A Note on Third Parties: When you perform a search, you are redirected to your chosen search engine (Google, Bing, etc.). HEAT Labs has no control over and accepts no responsibility for the data collection practices, privacy policies, or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of your selected search engine.
